# Classic recipe - ingredients
(4 - 6 servings)

## Meatballs
- 500 g (18 oz) ground (minced) beef/pork mixture
- 150 ml (4/5 cup) milk
- 75 g (¾ cup) white breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 onion
- salt, white pepper
- ground allspice

## Brown gravy
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp wheat flour
4 dl meat stock
½ dl double cream
2 tsp Chinese soy sauce
1 pinch black pepper
1 tbsp apple sauce or jelly (optional)
